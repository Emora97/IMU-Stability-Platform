# IMU-Stability-Platform
Design and fabrication of a servo actuated platform intended to maintain a stable platform during dynamic movement.


Using data from a BN055 IMU from Adafruit, two digital servos, and an Arduino Uno R3, a two gimbal system was designed to maintain tray position for up to 15 degrees of rotation along pitch and roll axis'.
Circuit design utilized a 4s LiPO battery and two separate buck converters to minimize overall weight and placement of batteries.
